Lab 8: Commuting to Dane County (2011-2015)

What to Expect?

Main Info

This is a flowmap showing the daily number of commuters traveling to Dane County, Wisconsin.

Thicker, darker-colored arrows show greater numbers of workers traveling to Dane County from their county of residence.

Thinner, lightly-colored arrows show fewer numbers of workers headed for Dane County.

Controls

At the top of the screen are four buttons: "Hide", "Show", "Pause", and "Play". They are all rather intuitive.

Click "Hide" to make the flowlines disappear.

Click "Show" to make the flowlines reappear.

Click "Pause" to halt the animation, and make the flowlines static.

Click "Play" to resume the animation.
