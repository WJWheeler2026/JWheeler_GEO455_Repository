Assignment Details

In this assignment, we are working with layer controls and plugins. We have three primary layers: street map, satellite imagery, and topographic map. Landmarks are based on Encyclopedia Britannica's entry on New Seven Wonders of the World and the images are linked from Wikimedia Commons. Included is a tracker for the International Space Station, along with a function that displays the coordinates of where you click on the map.
