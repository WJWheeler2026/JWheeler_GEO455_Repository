North Carolina Solar Explorer

An interactive map showcasing solar infrastructure, radiation potential, and suitable terrain across North Carolina.

About This Map

This web map integrates solar infrastructure, Direct Normal Irradiance (DNI), and terrain data to support spatial analysis of renewable energy development in one of America's leaders in solar energy. Data is designed and presented to be easily understood by students and planners.

Built using Leaflet, ArcGIS Pro and PHCode | GIS Data from USPVDB, NC OneMap, and NREL

Map Layers

🏭 Solar Projects

Locations of existing solar installations including utility-scale and distributed systems.

☀️ Direct Normal Irradiance

Measures solar radiation intensity, highlighting high-potential areas for energy generation.

⛰️ Shaded Relief

Terrain visualization that reveals elevation and landscape influences on solar siting.

How to Use

Click "Launch Map" to explore. Toggle layers, zoom into areas of interest, and interact with features for detailed information.
